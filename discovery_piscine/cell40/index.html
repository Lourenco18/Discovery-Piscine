Daniel and João

Web Developers

This website was created within the scope of Discovery Piscine's Rush project. The aim is to create a website using a template with the following requirements:
A navigation bar: You must implement your own navigation bar (with several links to move around your page easily). This bar must point to at least 3 parts of your summary. Finally this bar must remain fixed during your navigation on the page.
•Three parts: You must implement 3 parts in your summary.
•A first part : which will display simple content with a photo, a name and job title.
•A second part : which will display the professional experiences and skills of the person.
•A last part: with links to contact the person.
